1970 Matchbox Ford Mustang

Matchbox · 1970

This 1970 Matchbox Superfast Ford Mustang represents Matchbox's response to Hot Wheels' speed-focused designs. The Superfast line featured low-friction wheels that could compete with Hot Wheels on orange track.

This Mustang captures the muscle car era perfectly with its aggressive stance and detailed casting. It remains a favorite among collectors who appreciate both the Matchbox heritage and the iconic American muscle car design.
